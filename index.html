About Our Data Mission

Founded in 2018 in San Francisco, our agency emerged from a simple observation: most SEO companies were making decisions based on outdated practices and gut instinct rather than hard evidence. We stripped away the marketing jargon and built our foundation on data science, hiring statisticians alongside marketers to create a truly analytical approach to search engine visibility. What started as a small consultancy has grown into a premier partner for enterprise brands seeking transparency in their digital growth.

Our mission is to empower businesses with understandable, actionable, and transparent data insights. We believe that when clients understand the 'why' and 'how' behind their search performance, they can make bolder business decisions. Core to our values is radical honesty—if the data shows a strategy isn't working, we pivot immediately. We are committed to continuous learning, algorithmic adaptation, and delivering a Return on Investment that is clearly visible on your balance sheet, not just in vanity metrics.
